HTML5
========

## Nova sintaxe de atributo

Empty
`<input type="text" value="John Doe" disabled>`

Unquoted
`<input type="text" value=John>`

Double-quoted
`<input type="text" value="John Doe">`

Single-quoted
`<input type="text" value='John Doe'>`
